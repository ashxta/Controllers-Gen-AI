How It Works

Powered by Google Cloud AI
Advanced AI Analysis
Multi-layered detection system

TruthGuard uses Google Cloud's AI platforms including Natural Language API, Vision AI, and custom models trained on diverse misinformation patterns. Our system analyzes content through multiple layers including semantic analysis, sentiment manipulation detection, source credibility assessment, and cross-referencing with verified databases.

Real-Time Verification
Instant results with explanations

Our system processes content in real-time, providing not just verification results but detailed explanations of why content might be misleading. We identify specific manipulation techniques and provide context about the sources and claims being made.

About TruthGuard

Our mission
Our Mission
Combatting misinformation through education

TruthGuard was created to address the growing challenge of misinformation in digital spaces. Unlike traditional fact-checking services, we focus not just on verifying claims but on educating users about how misinformation works, empowering them to become more critical consumers of information.

Technology
Powered by Google Cloud AI

Our platform leverages Google Cloud's advanced AI capabilities including Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, and custom machine learning models trained on diverse datasets of verified and misleading information patterns.
